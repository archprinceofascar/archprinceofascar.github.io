Introduction

About the Archprincipality

The Archprincipality of Ascar (Äerzfürstentum vun Ascar) is a self-declared micronation with territorial claims in the United Kingdom, formally established in 2022. Ascar is governed as a semi-constitutional monarchy, with sovereign authority vested in the Archprince and legislative power exercised through the Imperial Council.

The nation takes its symbols — the crimson field and silver cross — from an ancient tradition of honour, faith, and order. Its institutions seek to embody the dignity and solemnity befitting a sovereign state.

Government

Governance & Constitution

Ascar is constituted as a semi-constitutional monarchy. The Archprince serves as head of state, holding sovereign authority over matters of state, foreign relations, and the conferral of honours. Legislative authority is vested in the Imperial Council, which serves as the nation's central governing body.
