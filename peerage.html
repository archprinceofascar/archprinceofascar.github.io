Editor's Preface

This Consolidated Edition of the Peerage of Ascar has been compiled under the authority of the Office of the Lord Herald, and is presented as a comprehensive record of all degrees of nobility extant within the realm. It is arranged in strict alphabetical order by family name. Titles are listed in descending order of precedence within each entry, with the most senior dignity appearing first. Honours and appointments of a personal nature are listed separately following the peerages.
